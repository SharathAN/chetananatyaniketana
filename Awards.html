Awards & Achievements

CNN is an institution which imparts training in Bharatanatyam. The school aims at providing training to all, irrespective of age, religion, caste, creed and the like. CNN have achieved a high level of proficiency in Bharatanatyam and won prizes, awards, titles, etc.

CNN has been honoured by awards:

"Nritya kala ratna 2009 & 2010"

"Basava Jayanthi – 2009"

"Mayura – 2008"

"Chetana Shri – 2007"

"Indirapriyadarshini – 2005"
